Overwatch 2 Was Released October 4th

Overwatch 2 is a videogame recently released on October 4th on PS4, PS5, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and PC. The game has recently critical acclaim for its diverse roster of various characters with numerous different playstyles, backgrounds, and stories. In just over a week after its lauch, Overwatch recorded over 25 million different players. All in all, it seems Overwatch 2 launch was a great success and the game will likely expand from here.
